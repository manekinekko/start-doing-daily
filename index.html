It's the year 2020 - The Future!

At Microsoft, our mission is to empower every person and organization on the planet to achieve more. In other words, to help you grow stronger and more confident in more aspects of life.

Not just technology!

Learning new ideas and skills around technology can help in many ways, and there are endless resources online to help everyone learn something new.

But what about the other stuff? What about the things that aren't found in Docs or in the new Microsoft Learn platform? What about "the people stuff"? The HARD stuff?

Start Doing - Daily is aimed at providing daily tips and suggestions to help everyone continuously improve the "other" aspects and skills required to achieve more.

On January 1st, 2020 we will begin releasing a new suggestion each day as we "Start" the new year.

Each post will include a suggestion to "Start doing" something that will greatly enable you to be a better developer, technologist, manager - in short a better person.
